# Sample Conversation 2 — Unavailable Item + Mid-Conversation Order Swap

Generated by `npm run demo:modification`. Demonstrates: the agent catching an
out-of-stock item and offering real alternatives instead of hallucinating
availability, and a single utterance that both cancels one item and adds
another ("actually cancel the fries, add a coke instead").

```
Customer: Can I get some fries
Steward:  Got it — 1 × Fries added to your order.

Customer: And a chicken seekh kebab please
Steward:  Got it — 1 × Chicken Seekh Kebab added to your order.

Customer: I'd also like the vegetable spring rolls
Steward:  Sorry, the Vegetable Spring Rolls is out of stock right now. Could I interest you in Samosa Chaat or Chicken Seekh Kebab instead?

Customer: What's in my order?
Steward:  So far you have: 1 × Fries (₹150), 1 × Chicken Seekh Kebab (₹320). Running total: ₹470.

Customer: Actually cancel the fries, add a coke instead
Steward:  Done — I've removed Fries from your order. Got it — 1 × Coke added to your order.

Customer: Is the seekh kebab spicy?
Steward:  The Chicken Seekh Kebab is medium spice. Minced chicken skewers grounded with ginger, garlic, and garam masala, char-grilled in the tandoor.

Customer: Place the order
Steward:  Great, confirming your order — total comes to ₹400. Sending it to the kitchen now!

--- Final Order State ---
  1 × Chicken Seekh Kebab — ₹320
  1 × Coke — ₹80
  Total: ₹400
```

Note: "Vegetable Spring Rolls" is marked `"availability": "out-of-stock"` in
`data/menu.json`. The agent's alternatives (Samosa Chaat, Chicken Seekh Kebab) are
pulled live from `checkAvailability`/`addToOrder`'s alternative-suggestion logic,
which filters the same category for in-stock items — nothing here is invented.
